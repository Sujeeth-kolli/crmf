Format for disclosure of TER of mutual fund schemes

  • Base TER excludes additional expenses provided in Regulation 52(6A)(b) and 52(6A)(c) of SEBI (Mutual Funds) Regulations, 1996 and goods & services tax on investment and advisory fees.
  • Additional Expenses not exceeding 0.30% in terms of Regulation 52(6A)(b) of SEBI (Mutual Funds) Regulations, 1996.
  • Additional Expenses not exceeding 0.05% in terms of Regulation 52(6A)(c) of SEBI (Mutual Funds) Regulations, 1996.
  • Present GST rate is 18%.
  • Percentages mentioned above are annualized.
  • Base TER of direct plan shall have a lower expense ratio excluding distribution expenses, commission, etc., and no commission shall be paid from such plans.
